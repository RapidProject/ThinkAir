Rapid mission

RAPID targets a novel heterogeneous CPU-GPU multi- level Cloud acceleration focusing on applications running on embedded systems of low-power devices. The project takes advantage of abundant mobile computation power and ubiquitous high-speed networks to provide a distributed heterogeneous acceleration infrastructure that can change the future of mobile applications.
